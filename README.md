# Miss Somali Pageant

Miss Somali is an international cultural pageant founded to celebrate Somali beauty, intelligence, and leadership on a global stage.

Established in 2025/2026 in Canada, the pageant provides a platform for Somali women to proudly represent their heritage while promoting unity, confidence, and humanitarian impact.

More than a competition, Miss Somali is a movement dedicated to empowering women through education, advocacy, and community engagement.

Official website: [https://misssomali.com/](https://misssomali.com/)
